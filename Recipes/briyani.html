Briyani Recipe

A very delightful dinner which comes from all day way from india.

Ingredients Needed

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Ghee
  • Curd

Steps to be followed

  1. Marinate chicken with curd and spice leave for an hour.
  2. Cook chicken 80%
  3. Put cooked rice in the same handi.
  4. Cover it up and let it cook.
  5. After 30 mins or so your biryani is ready to eat.
